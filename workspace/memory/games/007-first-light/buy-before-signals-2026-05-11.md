# 007 First Light reusable buy-before signals

Updated: 2026-05-11  
Use for: 玫玫物語買前必看 / 後續新手攻略前置流程

## Strong audience questions

- Is this actually a Hitman-style sandbox, or a cinematic action-adventure?
- How much freedom do missions have before funneling into set pieces?
- Is the disguise / outfit-stealing fantasy from Hitman present?
- Can players play stealth-first, or does it become Uncharted-style shooting?
- Is License to Kill a real gameplay rule?
- Are Q gadgets meaningful, and do they use resources?
- Is the young Bond convincing, or does he feel unlike James Bond?
- Should players buy PS5 / PC / Xbox at launch, or wait for Switch 2?
- Will physical/offline players be affected by Day One patch / online progression?
- Is TacSim enough to support replayability after the story?

## Evidence-backed chapter clusters

- Positioning: Hitman DNA + faster cinematic Bond action, not pure Hitman reskin.
- Gameplay: social stealth, eavesdropping, bluff, Q gadgets, License to Kill, melee/firearms.
- Purchase risk: platform timing, Switch 2 delay, console performance, PC specs, Day One patch/offline, version/preorder.
- Audience fit: suitable for single-player spy/action fans; risky for pure Hitman sandbox fans and physical/offline purists.

## Current fact locks

- PS5 / Xbox Series X|S / PC release: 2026-05-27.
- Switch 2: delayed to summer 2026, not same launch date.
- Single-player story-driven third-person action-adventure.
- Standalone reimagined Bond origin story, not based on a specific film.
- Steam TC page: Traditional Chinese interface/subtitles, no Traditional Chinese full audio.
- PC: 80GB storage; official specs and Steam should be rechecked before final script because recommended RAM display differs across pages.
